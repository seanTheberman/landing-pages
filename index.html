Why the assessment matters

A BER assessment is not just paperwork. It is the starting point for smarter property decisions.

A Building Energy Rating scores a home from A to G and gives buyers, tenants, lenders and grant bodies a clear energy-performance signal. For owners, it also highlights where money is being lost through heating, insulation, windows, ventilation or renewables.

What is a BER certificate?

A BER certificate is an official Building Energy Rating that shows how energy efficient a property is on an A to G scale.

Why do I need a BER assessment?

It is legally required for most property sales and rentals in Ireland, and it helps identify upgrade actions for comfort, bills and grant planning.

How does The Berman help?

The Berman lets you request quotes from local SEAI registered assessors, choose your preferred appointment, and then use advisor or catalogue routes for upgrades.

Can landlords use this?

Yes. Landlords can use BER certificates for rental compliance and to plan works that improve energy performance before or between tenancies.
